Distribution Rules

Equal-sharing-of-the-bonus rule (phase 1, rounds 1-4):
There is no differentiation in group bonus share. If the group threshold level is met,
each group member receives an equal share of the group bonus.

Equal-payoff rule (phase 2, round 5-8):
There is differentiation in group bonus share. Group members receive different shares of the group bonus
in such a way that every group member receives in principle the same payoff.
